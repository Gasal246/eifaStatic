EIFA - Exotic International Football Academy

EIFA Football Academy (EIFA) is a niche soccer academy headquatered in PALAKKAD, with AFC, AIFF, & FIFA qualified coaches We focus on the local talent pool, to identify the talented, passionated and committed and guide them to playing the highest level of soccer.

Sports Science Classes

Football is a complex sport requiring many factors to produce top footballers. We conduct monthly Sports Science Classes to explore the various facets needed to produce well-rounded footballers such as sports psycology, fitness, and tactics.

Weekly Technical Sessions

Technical Skills are the foundation of good football. The skills needed vary from one posision to other, with a Goalkeeper needed different skills Than a field player. Our weekly sessions work on Enlish FA's 5 Fundamentals of soccer: Dribbling, Passing, Shooting, Heading and Touch.

Weekly Tactical Sessions

Football often comes down to the strategy that a team employs at the end of close game. We examine and teach team tactics, Prepareness and Organisational skills needed to play effective football. Utilising UEFA designed 8-V-8 sessions, we deliver 90min sessions training our players in tactical principles of pressing, transition, counter attack and fluid movement.

Set Play Sessions

50% of goals are scored from set play. We organize a weekly session improving set play, freekicks, corners and even throw-ins.

Advanced Football Training

Eifa builds the most elite players through focusing equality on technical(Dribbling, Shooting, Crossing, Heading, Tacling), tactical strength and fitness and mental strength.

Incepted in March-2022 our mission at the football acdemy is to identify the best local football talent, ages 6-18 years old, devolop these talented players, and take them to the next level.

EIFA takes a unique approach into its programme, Using a successful formula of fitness, Technical and Tactical Sessions to devolop the complete football education. Each of our players is also given a weekly personal training program to further work on their specific fitnes and technical needs.

Our players are carefully selected and scouted with the aim of continuously developing a spirited team able to challenge for silverwares and produce world class players. EIFA is delighted to have the opportunity of working with KERALA's fitness players.

Nutrition

When not in the Gym or training field young players ned to be able to recover from the effort and avoid injuries. A healthy body is a healthy athlete that is why one of our priorities is to inform and educate the importance of nutrition. Adopting of a healthy lifestyle is a key element of our more educational approach to football and we want to ensure all our players have a clear understanding of the 'good' and 'bad' in food.

OUR MISSION

The football Academy was formed to povide the oppertunities for youth elite soccer players to train and complete in KERALA and Internationaly.

Our mission is to devolop soccer skills as well as maintain an environment that embodies the core values of integrity, leadership, Perseverence, respect, and Teamwork while enhancing self-esteem, Promoting self Confidence and cultivating a life long pattern for the beautiful game of soccer.

While our teams strive to complete at the highest levels of completion, EIFA is fully committed to player devolopment.

We believe that a commitment to player devolopment establishes a solid technical and mental foundation for which to continue building upon at EIFA and beyond.

We intented to accomplish the mission with experienced coaching staff that represent the most effective and comprehensive club soccer program in dubai.

OUR VISSION

  • Develop student/athletes who are committed to personal excellence and team success.

  • Provide an inspirational and motivational environment where preparation, practice, and hard work generate legitimate opportunities for success.

  • Emphasize the importance and development of fundamentals and techniques necessary for positive experiences in football and in life.

  • Develop well-disciplined student-athletes who strive for personal, educational and athletic excellence both on the field and in the community.
